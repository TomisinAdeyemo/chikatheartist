WHO I AM

I’m Chika the hands behind Chika the artist.I work mainly with traditional media, mixing paint, and found textures to create pieces that feel alive,  and a little bit trippy. My art balances playfulness and abstraction. Each piece is beautiful place between control and surrender, curiosity and instinct, I think most of all that’s what I love about it, I’m communicating my mind, thoughts and my roots and inviting you to share a piece of it.
